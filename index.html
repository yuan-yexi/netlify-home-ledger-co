How to Get It Going

Like all things worth doing, you'll need a plan, a strategy.

Financial Position

You can start off by gathering these data points:

  • Your CPF and cash positions will play a large part in the approcah you should take to acquiring the right property. Log onto the CPF website to easily access this data.
  • Listing down any outstanding loans and liabilities. This will determine the leverage that your can maximize with banks. You can get this information from your bank.

Understand your Goals, the Market and Data

  • Research property value and trends by individual districts. This will help you understand the upside and risks involved.
  • Research on the mortgage options - a mortgage or financial calculator will be a great help.
  • Optimize your goals and budget. While having leverage is advantagous, it is important with any investment you undertake to not overstretch your financial positions.

Plan Out Your Strategy and Roadmap

Create a plan and execute. A plan provides more then a list of to-dos, it should also serve as a yardstick to ensure you're on the right track.

  • Once your goals are set and have an ideal location and property in mind put it down
  • Get out there and start hunting!
